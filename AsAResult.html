Bluebird

Bluebird awoke in an abandoned lab, equipment dry rotted and broken. She eventually trained at Astarne academy, with Team Valiant, and joined the rebellion against the chaos faction. Her team travels the universe in search of the primal mages in order to restore peace to all worlds. Bluebird also has another goal, saving the other birds from the man who made them, the doctor.

Saberwing

A newer recruit to the rebellion, Saberwing made a distasteful impression when sent on a mission to the rebellion ship. He was still under the influence of the doctor at the time. After he was subdued, his mind severed the connection between him and the doctor. Now, he joins the fight against the chaos faction in search of his past alongside Bluebird.

Flamingo

Flamingo remembers very little of her time before the rebellion, but longs for Hummingbird to be reunited with her. She, as Cordelia, went looking for him when he went missing some years ago, only to turn up missing herself. Little did she know, she did indeed find him.

Canary

Not much is know about this bird. She is rarely seen in combat, but is known for her electricity magic. Bluebird knew her when she was Ava, they were both captive together, but she has little memory outside of that.

Hummingbird

This bird is still under the hold of the doctor. Not much is known about his life prior to the experimentation, but his recent mission had been taking the other birds back to the doctor for reassembly. He only succeeded in grabbing one, Bluebird. The team is now on a mission to recover her.
